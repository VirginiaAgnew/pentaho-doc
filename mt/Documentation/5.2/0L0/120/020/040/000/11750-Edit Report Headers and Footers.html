To create the page headers, double-click the upper-left or upper-right corner of the report.

A pop-up dialog box appears. You can:

  • Delete any existing words, or enter a new header and press <ENTER >.
  • Click Date and press <ENTER > to insert a date and time stamp in the page header. You can customize the date and page count by adding your own text, such as, Today: $(report.date), instead of using the default, $(report.date) .
  • Click #/Pages and press <ENTER > to insert the number of pages in the report, as in 1/1 (page one of one).

Repeat this process for the page footers, at bottom-left and bottom-right.
