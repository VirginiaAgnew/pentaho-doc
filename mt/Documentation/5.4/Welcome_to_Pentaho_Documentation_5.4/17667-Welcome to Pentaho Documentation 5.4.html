Overview

Learn what's new about the Pentaho Documentation Center.

Welcome to the Pentaho Documentation Center, powered by MindTouch. The wiki-like authoring environment allows for real-time updates and encourages community collaboration. Users receive up-to-date content since authors can make improvements and changes quickly. In addition, registered members can provide feedback on topics, leave comments, and engage with the authors of the content, resulting in immediate and effective updates and changes.

Get started by registering

To make the most of this dynamic documentation center, we encourage you to register on the site .

While all users can save an article as a PDF and email article links to colleagues, registered users have the power to provide feedback on articles and to leave comments and questions for the authors. Registered users will receive notifications when favorite articles are updated and when questions and comments are addressed.  

To register, click the Sign In link at the top of this page. 

Feature Highlights

These enhancements will assist you in finding the answers to your questions.

Filtered Search Results

  • Our 5.4 release now features filtered search results. You can drill down while searching to find the exact topic you need in the version and category you want.
  • Contextual searching from the page you are on when you enter a search term.

Search Recommendations

  • We tag our content to return recommended search results to save you time.

Seamless navigation

  • Many articles feature a table of contents so you can see all the sections at a glance.

  • Use the Previous and Next buttons to ready articles sequentially.

  • Easy-to-navigate breadcrumbs let you know where you are in the site so you can easily jump to other articles.

User-friendly look and feel

  • A snapshot view of the most popular and recently updated articles on the site.

  • Ability to browse by popular tasks on the main page for quick answers to frequently asked questions.

  • Links to other Pentaho resources, such as the Forums, Support, and Professional Services.
