You can edit prompts as needed. Use these steps to edit prompts.

  1. Locate the prompts pane at the top of the panes in the dashboard.
  2. Located the different prompts and the drop-down menus next to them.
  3. Select the prompt you want to edit and choose an alternative prompt from the drop down menu. The prompt changes, causing the graphs and charts in the dashboard to change appropriately.
