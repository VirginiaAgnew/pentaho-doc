Overview

Explains what to do after you have successfully upgraded the software.

After you have tested the upgrade, perform post-upgrade tasks. These include:

  • Reviewing the minor functionality changes that could impact upgrade.
  • Configuring the DI Server .
  • Reading  Administer DI Server .
  • Deprovisioning the old server and provisioning the new one in accordance with your organization's procedures.
