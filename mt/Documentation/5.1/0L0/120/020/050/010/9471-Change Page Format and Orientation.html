By default, Interactive Report creates a portrait mode page in Letter format (8.5" x 11"), a format used in the United States. You may need to change the page orientation or format.

To change page orientation, click the General tab, then click Page Setup . In the Page Setup dialog box, click the appropriate icon. Click OK to save your changes.

To change page format, click the General tab, then click Page Setup . In the Page Setup dialog box, choose the correct page format for your report. Page margin measurements are displayed in the lower part of the dialog box. You can adjust the measurements as needed. Click OK to save your changes.
