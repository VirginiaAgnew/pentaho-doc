Learn what's new about the Pentaho Documentation Center.

Welcome to the new Pentaho Documentation Center, powered by MindTouch. The wiki-like authoring environment allows for real-time updates and encourages community collaboration. Users receive up-to-date content since authors can make improvements and changes quickly. In addition, registered members can provide feedback on topics, leave comments, and engage with the authors of the content, resulting in immediate and effective updates and changes.

Get started by registering!

To make the most of this dynamic documentation center, we encourage you to register on the site. While all users can save an article as a PDF and email article links to colleagues, registered users have the power to provide feedback on articles and to leave comments and questions for the authors. Registered users will receive notifications when favorite articles are updated and when questions and comments are addressed.  

To register, click the Sign In link at the top of this page. 

Feature Highlights

These enhancements will assist you in finding the answers to your questions.

Robust searching within the documentation center

  • Tagged content returns more focused search results.

  • Advanced search tools improve filtering of results.

Seamless navigation

  • Each article includes Previous and Next buttons for a more seamless online reading experience.

  • Easy-to-navigate breadcrumbs let you know where you are in the site so you can easily jump to other articles.

  • Detailed articles feature an internal table of contents so you can skip to the section you're looking for.

User-friendly look and feel

  • A snapshot view of the most popular and recently updated articles on the site.

  • Ability to browse by popular tasks on the main page for quick answers to frequently asked questions.

  • Links to other Pentaho resources, such as the Forums, Support, and Professional Services.
